## Positioning

Devin Wilson / **DW Tailored Systems** builds the specific internal tools that
operations teams need but nobody's selling off the shelf. Three distinct
audiences, one practice.

## Segments

### 1. Research Tools — Research & biotech automation
- **Audience:** Lab directors, PIs, ops leads at small-to-mid research orgs
  (academic cores, biotech startups, genomics/diagnostics labs, 5–50 people)
- **Pain:** Manual sample tracking, spreadsheet LIMS, instrument data wrangling,
  regulatory paper trails eating bench time
- **Unfair angle:** Insider lab workflow knowledge + full-stack + AI —
  most vendors sell generic LIMS or the team writes fragile Python scripts
- **Primary outcome to sell:** Hours back at the bench, audit-ready data
- **Engagement shape:** 4–8 week custom builds, project-based pricing

### 2. Websites — Brand & marketing sites for operators
- **Audience:** Founders and small ops-heavy businesses (restaurants,
  service businesses, pre-Series A startups) who need a real site, not a
  templated Squarespace
- **Pain:** DIY sites that don't convert, agencies that over-design and
  under-ship, no one owning the integration with their CRM / booking / etc.
- **Unfair angle:** Design + engineering + ops literacy in one person —
  site ships connected to the tools they actually use
- **Primary outcome to sell:** A site that works as a business asset, not a
  brochure
- **Engagement shape:** 2–6 week fixed-scope projects

### 3. Business Tools — Internal tools & AI workflows for SMBs
- **Audience:** Operators at 10–200 person businesses (restaurants, logistics,
  professional services) where spreadsheets + generic SaaS have hit a ceiling
- **Pain:** Process lives in one person's head, reporting is copy-paste,
  repetitive work that should be automated isn't
- **Unfair angle:** I embed with the team, learn the actual process, and
  build exactly what they need — no off-the-shelf compromise
- **Primary outcome to sell:** Specific bottleneck removed, time saved,
  decisions made with better data
- **Engagement shape:** Discovery → 4–8 week build → ongoing retainer optional
